Swedish Meatballs

How to make Swedish Meatballs

Sweidsh meatballs are some of the best tasting meatballs you'll ever taste. The creamy sauce paired with the meat and noodles make for an amazing combination.

The secret to making the best tasting Swedish Meatballs is all in the spices. This recipe will show you how to make them.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parsley
  • Allspice and Nutmeg
  • Onion
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Egg
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Beef Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Dijon Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix in medium sized bowl. Combine ground beef, panko, parsley, all spice, nutmeg, onion, garlic, powder, salt and pepper and egg. Mix till combined
  2. Roll into 12 large meatballs or 20 small meatballs. In a large skillet heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter.
  3. Brown. Add the meatballs and cook turning continuously until brown on each side and cooked through. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil.
  4. Sauce. In the same skillet add 4 tablespoons butter and flour and whisk till it turns brown. Slowly stir in beef broth and heavy cream. Add the worcestershire sauce and mustart and bring to a simmer till it starts to thicken.
  5. Simmer. Add the meatballs back to the skillet and simmer for another 1-2 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
